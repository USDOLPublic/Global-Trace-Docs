Global Trace

2. Role definition

The super administrator user must define each data input role required in the system, and the functionality or critical tracking events that they should be able to record.

Each role consists of a name, role type and list of permissions. There is a subset of permissions that can be selected for each of the role types. there are four role types: administrator role; producer role; ESG assessor role; and brand role.

Administrator role configuration

Listed below is a table of all permissions that can be assigned for administrator roles.

Category / Permission Action
Onboarding Complete user profile information when first creating their account
Synthetic DNA test results Log Synthetic DNA test results / view Synthetic DNA test results for products in the supply chain
Generate QR codes Administer QR codes by creating groups of codes, downloading them, and sending them to suppliers. QR Codes are on of the data carriers supported in Global trace
Submit incident reports Create/View Incident report & assign to Information partners (ESG assessor roles)
User Management Inviting/managing users on the system, view risk assessment of producer roles
Trace product Create an order & trace product using product identifiers and other attributes like Invoice number/Packing list number through their supply chain

Producer role configuration

A Producer role is any user that purchases, transforms, and sells products. When defining a producer, the super administrator must also specify if the facility extracts raw material, and the chain of custody model that will be supported.

Category / Permission Action
Onboarding (Profile) Complete user profile information when first creating their account. This can be configured to be undertaken by the user themselves, or onboarding by the administrator user.
Onboarding (SAQ) User must complete an assessment during onboarding. The assessment can be uploaded by the administrator, or the user can complete themselves (as a self assessment questionnaire).
Invite partners Add other business partners for recording transaction/shipping activities
Log purchase Record purchase information. The super administrator must specify: the data carriers supported (product ID, QR code, DNA marker ID); support provided for imported products; and if the user can record purchase from intermediaries(brokers)
Log transformations Recording transformation activity and assign product identifier for output product
Log sale Record sale transaction to another business partner
Log by-product Record quantity of by-product generated during transformation process
Log transport Record transport of products from the current user to another user
View History Overview of all product activities(transaction/transformation/shipping) and by-product along with margin of error
Trace product Create an order & trace product using product identifiers and other objects like Invoice number/Packing list number through their supply chain

Producer roles can be identified using standardized identifiers. In the onboarding phase of the current version of Global Trace, producer roles must specify an Open Supply Hub Identifier (OS ID). The complete process of configuring Open Supply Hub is included in the sub-page.

ESG Assessor role configuration

This role is used to define Information partners who assess conditions in facilities and report them. Assessment can be undertaken reactively (after receiving a request for investigation), or proactively (after undertaking a community risk scan.

Category / Permission Action
Onboarding (Profile) Complete profile by themselves or admin
Onboarding (SAQ) Assign an SAQ to be completed during account creation. This SAQ can be configured to be undertaken by the user themselves, or by the administrator user.
Submit reports (Proactive assessments) Proactively submit incident reports on labor issues against any facility via Community Risk Scan
Submit reports (Reactive assessments) Reactively responds to incident reports assigned to them
View Reports Ability to view either all incident reports or only reports assigned to myself

##Brand role configuration This role is used to define brand who can use this platform for managing their suppliers and trace product throughout their supply chain

Category / Permission Action
Onboarding (Profile) Complete profile
Onboarding (SAQ) Assign an SAQ to be completed during account creation. This SAQ can be configured to be undertaken by the user themselves, or by the administrator user.
Trace product Create an order & trace product using product identifiers and other objects like Invoice number/Packing list number all the way through their supply chain to find the origin
Supplier Management Manage their own suppliers and view comprehensive risk assessment of them
