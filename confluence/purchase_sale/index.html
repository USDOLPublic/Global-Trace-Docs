Appendix D. Purchases and sales

Only users who have a product role can perform sales and purchase products in the system.

Purchase

The user role must have permission LOG_PURCHASE and appear in the Supply Chain Map to use this feature.

  1. When a user wants to purchase products from a seller, which is a facility and is called a supplier, this seller must be the partner of his/her facility.
  • The type of seller and buyer’s facilities must be represented in one node of the Supply Chain Map.
  • Example: A user of the spinner facility wants to purchase products from a seller - the ginner facility, there must be a node with data of {role: Spinner, fromRole: Ginner} in the Supply Chain Map. If the seller is not represented in the Supply Chain Map, this seller is considered an intermediary (broker). The user role must have the permission ALLOW_PURCHASE_INTERMEDIARIES to perform this action.
  1. The user can also purchase products from no seller. To perform this action, his/her facility type must have the chain of custody MASS_BALANCE and appear in the second node - the next node of the initial node (the first node) of the Supply Chain Map.

There is another flow called “adding product manually”. Users can import products that are not tracked from raw material extraction using Global Trace. This would represent the situation where a supplier purchases input cotton from a producer who is not registered on the system.

Purchase from seller

  • Purchased products must have the same product definition.
  • Purchased products must be represented in a node of the Supply Chain Map.
  • Sold products can not be purchased.

After the transaction is completed, the purchased products are assigned to the buyer’s facility.

Add product manually

  • Added products must have the same product definition.
  • Attributes and their values of products are validated by the product definition.

Note

Note: There are three special attributes whose values are extracted and saved directly to the product for convenient access.

  • The value of the attribute of type PRODUCT_ID is the product code.
  • The value of the first attribute of type PRODUCT_QUANTITY is the product quantity and its quantity unit is the product quantity unit.
  • The values of attributes of category ATTACHMENTS are the product certifications.

Sale

The user role must have permission LOG_SALE and appear in the Supply Chain Map to use this feature. When a user wants to sell products to a buyer, this buyer must be the partner of his/her facility. The type of seller and buyer’s facilities must be represented in a node of the Supply Chain Map.
